Welcome Students and Parents

Professor McGonagall

I am looking forward to working with the young witches and wizards of this upcoming year. Students will be fully familiar and capable in Transfiguration by the time of graduation. I am excited to bring students to their full potential. Please refer to the Syllabus to see what students will be learning in detail.

I am open for students who need extra help after class on Tuesdays and Thursdays anywhere from 4:00PM-6:00PM. Extra help sessions need to be scheduled with me beforehand.

For those placed in the Gryffindor house, I will be your supervisor for your entire Hogwarts career. Please do not be afraid to speak with me if something is of trouble to you. I will do my best to bring you all to success during your time here at Hogwarts and prepare you for life.

If you have any questions, you can contact me via letter or come to my office after class hours.

Transfiguration

Transfiguration is some of the most complex and dangerous magic you will learn at Hogwarts. Anyone messing around in my class will leave and not come back. You have been warned.

Transfiguration is the magic when changing the form and appearance of an object. It is possible to turn inanimate objects into animate ones and vice versa. Some Transfiguration spells can alter parts of an object. Learning to become an Animagus is also possible, but is a difficult higher magic.

To contact Hogwarts or send letters to students, postage should be sent by owl mail.
