Welcome!

Hagrid

I am excited to work with the next class of young witches and wizards of the next few years. I teach students the Care for Magical Creatures and help them become familiar with them. It is important to learn and understand how these creatures work, especially in the case that one might encounter one in the wild. Please look at the Syllabus to see the overview of creatures that will be discussed and interacted with.

I am open for student visits before and after class hours anytime before curfew. If you have any questions for me, you can contact me via letter or drop by to talk.

To contact Hogwarts or send letters to students, postage should be sent by owl mail.
