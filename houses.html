The Houses

The Four Houses date all the way back to the founding of Hogwarts in the 10th century. Each student will be sorted into one of the four Houses in their first year by Sorting Hat. The House a student is sorted into reflects the strongest and deepest points of their character. This is a special occassion for many, seeing as how the house one is placed into becomes family at Hogwarts.

Gryffindor

Founder: Godric Gryffindor
Mascot: Lion
Colors: Scarlet & Gold
Head: Minerva McGonagall
House Ghost: Nearly Headless Nick
Traits: Courage, Chivalry, Nerve

Slytherin

Founder: Salazar Slytherin
Mascot: Serpent
Colors: Emerald & Silver
Head: Serverus Snape
House Ghost: The Bloody Baron
Traits: Ambition, Cunning, Leadership

Hufflepuff

Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
Mascot: Badger
Colors: Yellow & Black
Head: Pomona Sprout
House Ghost: The Fat Friar
Traits: Hardworking, Patience, Loyalty

Ravenclaw

Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw
Mascot: Eagle
Colors: Blue & Bronze
Head: Filius Flitwick
House Ghost: The Grey Lady
Traits: Intelligence, Creativity, Wit

To contact Hogwarts or send letters to students, postage should be sent by owl mail.
